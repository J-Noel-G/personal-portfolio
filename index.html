About Me

Hi there! I'm Noel Garcia, currently serving as an Emergency Manager for the Air Force at MacDill AFB. My role involves critical planning and response, but my true passion lies in the dynamic world of technology and cybersecurity.

I hold a degree in Cybersecurity and am continuously expanding my expertise. I've earned my **Security+** certification and am always pursuing additional certifications to deepen my knowledge and skills. My goal is to leverage my unique blend of emergency management experience and technical acumen to break into the cybersecurity field.

I believe in hands-on learning and practical application. Outside of my military service, I dedicate my time to experimenting with new technologies, building out my home lab, and exploring innovative solutions in network security and system administration.
